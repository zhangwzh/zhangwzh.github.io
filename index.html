Curriculum Vitae (updated: January 2020)

I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics at University of Washington, advised by Profs. Daniela Witten and Yen-Chi Chen. I also serve as a research intern at Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Han Liu. I obtained my B.Sc. in Mathematics and B.Econ. in Finance from Sun Yat-sen University.

My research interests lie in the interface between statistics, machine learning and optimization, especially including statistical machine learning, high-dimensional statistics, causal inference, stochastic optimization, graphical models, etc.
